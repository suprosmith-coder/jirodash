Help Cyanix Improve

Make Cyanix smarter for everyone

When you opt in, your conversations are automatically cleaned, improved, and anonymized before being stored. This creates a high-quality dataset that helps Cyanix give better answers over time -- not just for you, but for all users.

Contribute to training
Anonymized + cleaned versions of your chats help improve Cyanix
How your data is used
Prompt Refiner
Your message is rewritten to be clear and specific before storing
Response Improver
The AI response is polished and structured before being saved
Quality Filter
Only high-quality, toxicity-free exchanges are kept. Your feedback votes count toward this.
Withdraw contributions
Remove all your anonymized data from the training dataset

All stored data is fully anonymized -- emails, URLs, and personal identifiers are removed before storage. Cyanix never sells data. You can withdraw at any time.

Privacy Policy

Cyanix AI stores your chat history and memories in Supabase to power your personalized experience. We do not sell your data. You can delete your memories and chats at any time from Settings.
